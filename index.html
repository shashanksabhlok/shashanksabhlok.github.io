ECE 155: Embedded Systems & Design



ECE 155 is the first embedded systems course for Electrical and Computer Engineering Students at the University of Waterloo. Along with giving students an oppurtunity to program basic embedded devices, this course also gives them an idea of the best software practices used in Industry today.The labs, which are an integral part of this class, start off with displaying basic graphs on an android device (e.g Acceleration v/s Time), go on and ask students to devise a pedometer and eventually culminate into a simple Android navigation app that trails a person's path across a room(image above).Check it out on Github.
